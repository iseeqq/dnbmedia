ABOUT THE LABEL

DNB MEDIA is more than just a record label; it is the true epicenter of a new sound in Kazakhstan. We specialize in promoting high-quality electronic and bass music, uniting those who live by the rhythm.

Our mission is to build a powerful, independent platform for local talent, opening up new horizons in the industry. We break stereotypes and prove that the local scene can deliver a world-class sound, tearing up dancefloors and sound systems alike.

OUR HISTORY

The foundation of the label was laid in 2022. At the helm of DNB MEDIA stands an ambitious musician and visionary — Zhainak Ai Sultan Rinatuly, widely known in the electronic music scene by his stage name ISEEQ.

Starting his journey with a deep, genuine love for music and breakbeat culture, iseeq realized that the Kazakhstani scene lacked a unified center of gravity. That's how the idea of DNB MEDIA was born. In a short amount of time, the label evolved from a bold concept into a fully operational machine generating heavy bass and tight beats, bringing the founder's unique vision to the masses.
